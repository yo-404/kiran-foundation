Essential Finance Skills For Women - 2022

CA Viheta Rathore & CA Anant Pankaj Jain

Kiran foundation presents "Essential Finance Skills For Women", this is a special course designed with essential information about money and finance, which every women of this nation should be aware of. This course covers topic such as Banking System, Basic Behavorial Economics, Savings, Overview of various Investment Options, Investing in Gold, Importance of Insurance, Preparing a useful will etc. The course will last 8 weeks from 21st May 2022 to 9th July 2022. Classes will be every Saturday at 3:30 PM IST. We will try to answer all your queries during the course. The participants who complete all classes will also receive a certifacte at the end of the course.
