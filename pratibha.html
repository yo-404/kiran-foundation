Kiran Pratibha

Kiran Foundation offers scholarship to exceptional but underprivileged youth in their endeavours of Education, Sports or Art, according to their interest and abilities. Foundation will be offering active guidance and personalised mentoring along with Financial Support. The sole aim of foundation is be to make this kids capable and successful.


Apply

What we offfer



Financial Support

The foundation will provide complete financial support to aid the scholar towards his/her persuite. This includes fee, accomodation, travel, puchase of books, equipments etc.

Personal Mentoring

Our team at foundation will work closely with each child. The team will gather nacessary resources and connections. Each child will be provided a personal mentor, who will be available for support at any given time.

Selection



Eligibility

  • Monthly income of the Family should be less than Rs. 20,000.
  • Child should have passed 8th Standard or 14 Years of age.
  • Child should have shown enormous potential in his/her field of interest.

Selection Process

  • Application Submitted by the applicant
  • Application filtering based on documents
  • Due diligence and background checks
  • Telephonic Inrerview
  • Home Visit
  • Decision Announced by the committee
