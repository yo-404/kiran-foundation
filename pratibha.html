Kiran Pratibha

Kiran Foundation offers scholarships to exceptional but underprivileged youth in their endeavors of Education, Sports or Art, according to their interest and abilities. The foundation will be offering active guidance and personalized mentoring along with Financial Support. The sole aim of the foundation is to make these kids capable and successful.


NOTE: Applications for Pratibha 2023 are now closed. If you have applied for Pratibha 2023 and would like to edit your application, please contact us. We have started the selection process and our team will reach out to you for further information. The results for the selection will be announced on our website in 2-3 weeks.


FAQ

What we offfer



Financial Support

The foundation will provide complete financial support to aid the scholar in his/her pursuit. This includes fees, accommodation, travel, purchase of books, equipment etc.

Personal Mentoring

Our team at foundation will work closely with each child. The team will gather nacessary resources and connections. Each child will be provided a personal mentor, who will be available for support at any given time.

Selection



Eligibility

  • Monthly income of the Family should be less than Rs. 20,000.
  • Child should have passed 8th Standard or 14 Years of age.
  • Child should have shown enormous potential in his/her field of interest.

Selection Process

  • Application Submitted by the applicant
  • Application filtering based on documents
  • Due diligence and background checks
  • Telephonic Inrerview
  • Home Visit
  • Decision Announced by the committee
