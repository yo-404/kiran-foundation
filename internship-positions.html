About Internship

Who we are ?

We are Kiran Foundation - a non-profit small "team" supporting underprivileged students and empowering women. If a hardworking, talented youth can not fulfil his dreams due to lack of resources, it is a failure of the whole society. We dream of a society where merit and hard work is supported irrespective of the financial, physical or social status. Kiran Foundation is a family of like minded, capable and compassionate people and we are committed to bring a fundamental shift in our society.


What we do ?

Well, a few things.

  • We provide support to talented students coming from financially weak backgrounds. We aim to provide them financial support and direct mentorship during their higher education.
  • We also work for women empowerment by educating them about technology, finance, law and English language. More details can be found at our website: kiran.foundation


    • What do we offer ?

    Our Internships are unpaid, but we do guarantee a very enriching and transformative experience along with complete Coaching/Mentoring for your career and personal growth. We also provide you Certificate, Training, Recommendation Letter, Agile Environment.
    All out of pocket expenses (including food, travel and dining) incurred for the purpose of internship are reimbursed.
