Why learn LAW FOR WOMEN

Kiran Foundation presents "Everyday Law For Women", which is a special course designed with essential legal information, which every woman of this nation should be aware of. This course covers topics such as sexual harassment in the workplace, right to private defense, domestic violence, right to free legal aid, maternity benefits, right to get an equal share in the inheritance, and about national commision for women. The course will last three weeks, with two classes every week on Wednesday and Friday at 2:00 p.m. We will try to answer all your queries during the course. The participants who complete all 6 classes will also receive a certificate at the end of the course.
