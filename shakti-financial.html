Why learn EFS

Kiran Foundation presents "Essential Finance Skills For Women", this is a special course designed with essential information about money and finance, which every woman of this nation should be aware of. This course covers topics such as the Banking System, Basic Behavioral Economics, Savings, Overview of various Investment Options, Investing in Gold, the Importance of Insurance, Preparing a useful will, etc. The course will last 8 weeks from 21st May 2022 to 9th July 2022. Classes will be every Saturday at 3:30 PM IST. We will try to answer all your queries during the course. The participants who complete all classes will also receive a certificate at the end of the course.
